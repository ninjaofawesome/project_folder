An abbreviated field guide on HTML, CSS and Sparrows.

Hosted by soon-to-be famous Ornithological Coders David Gabeau, Tessa Fallon and Hannah Nordgren.

On the first day of Flatiron School Brooklyn we took two hours to make an HTML static website for our team of three members. Our website includes a header for our team name "Arrows For Sparrows," links to our learning sections and bio pages on a sidebar, and our comprehensive body of Sparrow work in the body.
