Exciting Sparrow Fact No. 4: Hygiene and Modesty

Bathing in public. Sparrows have no shame. Is this a rejection of puritanical attitudes towards nudity? Or are they exhibitionists? A question for the philosophers. Sparrows will bathe anywhere, any time. They will also bathe in dirt, just to be peverse.
